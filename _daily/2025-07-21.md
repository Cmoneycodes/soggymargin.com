# Pain of Reality

## The world caught my attention

As most believe, you need to pay bills. I got a job, neglected my blog, crypto podcast, and my internet vibe. I was lazy and didn't believe hard enough. I wasn't willing to move anywhere with an internet connection. Typing that in my head sounds like a remote island with Starlink, but after I finish typing a dream I envision reality as a small concrete box with a singular window sitting atop the far right wall from the door. A mattress lies in the far corner. How pitiful. Even now I am in a creative cage as I am using punctuation.
